About Us

WATER CIRCULAR TECH INVENTS (PTY) LTD Established in 2021 and registered with Registration Number 2021/563298/07

WATER CIRCULAR TECH INVENTS (PTY) LTD is a private company registered in South Africa and BEE compliant. The company is led by experts in the field of water quality monitoring and wastewater treatment. The company currently provides exclusively water treatment services for customers such as municipalities and mining companies, ensuring that their wastewaters meet the requirement for discharge into the environment.

Objectives

To provide solutions to water pollution issues in South Africa and the rest of Africa, To provide reliable wastewater analysis and treatment, to make profit, To acquire a strong consulting team that will provide advice for all water issues.

Mission

To become a leading water company that will contribute to improve access to clean water in the continent and grow financially.

Keys to Success

Provide reliable services to our customers ,Maintain a good customer relationship, Improve our performance over the year and extent our area of practice.
